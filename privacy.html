Privacy Policy for DietraScan Tracker

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

Welcome to DietraScan Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice or our practices with regard to your personal information, please contact us at support.dietrascan@gmail.com.

When you use our mobile application (the "App"), we appreciate that you are trusting us with your personal information. In this privacy notice, we describe our privacy policy.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the App, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, or otherwise when you contact us.

Information you provide:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password.
  • Health and Dietary Information: Caloric intake, nutritional goals, body metrics (such as weight and height), and dietary preferences.
  • Device Access: With your explicit permission, we access your device's camera and photo library strictly for the purpose of scanning food labels.

Information automatically collected:

  • Log and Usage Data: Service-related, diagnostic, usage, and performance information our servers automatically collect when you access or use our App (e.g., Crashlytics logs).
  • Device Data: Information about your device, such as IP address, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

We use personal information collected via our App for a variety of business purposes described below:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • To provide our core services: Analyzing food labels via AI to extract nutritional data.
  • To manage user accounts: Allowing you to track your daily nutrition, view history, and generate PDF export reports.
  • To improve our App: Analyzing usage trends to improve user experience.

3. Will Your Information Be Shared With Anyone?

We only share information with the following third parties to perform essential functions on our behalf:

  • Firebase (Google): For secure user authentication (login/signup), real-time database storage (Firestore), and crash reporting (Crashlytics).
  • Google Gemini API: For processing the text extracted from images of food labels. Note: Images are used transiently to extract text and are not permanently stored by our servers.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We will only keep your personal information for as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy notice, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

When you choose to delete your account using the in-app "Delete Account" feature, your personal data, dietary logs, and authentication records are permanently and immediately erased from our live servers and your local device.

5. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, despite our safeguards and efforts to secure your information, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to the personal information we collect from you, change that information, or delete it in some circumstances. You can review, change, or terminate your account at any time by logging into the App settings.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this notice, you may email us at support.dietrascan@gmail.com.
